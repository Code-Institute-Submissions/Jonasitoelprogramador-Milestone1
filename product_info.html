The Green Machine

What is it?

Dark and indulgent, The Kalamata Machine is the next stop on the Tapenade Taste Tour (trademarked so don't you even think about it). The deep earthy notes of the Kalamata olive really come through here creating a rich paste both in taste and texture. The addition of aubergine brings an extra creamy quality, however, the sun-dried tomatoes give it enough bounce to still spring out of the jar. A must-have for tapenade-enthusiasts who love classic-tasting tapenade but aren't afraid to mix things up from time to time.
