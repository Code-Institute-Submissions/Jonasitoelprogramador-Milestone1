Our Story

Our journey (if you'll pardon the pun) began as most good ones do: on holiday in 2002 with a couple of mates and a variety of mediterranean olive-based spreads. These two friends (now the co-owners of Journeyman) tasted tapenade for the first time on this holiday and never looked back. The simply counld not comprehend how such an effortlessly delicious (not to mention juicy) foodstuff had evaded detection by mainstream culinary institutions for so long. "Not for long!" they both proclaimed simultaneously into the Provançal night and, over some unpleasant rosé that they had purchased that morning at tourists prices, they agreed that this affront to tastebuds everywhere should be put to rights. The next day, after various hangover cures had been hastily concocted and ingested, Journeyman was born! - a company dedicated soley to the disemination of tapenade knowledge and products for the good of mankind.
